Second Half,
Week 4

Data analysis

We discovered that players read the instructions and found them helpful, but that most did not entirely understand how to play simply after reading the instructions. But, most people eventually understood how to play after a little bit of experience with the game.

Projectile was the favorite spell, and no one found wall to be the most effective, which supports previous playtesting feedback that the wall doesn't have a well-defined purpose.

We previously received feedback that the game was too long, but we didn't get that feedback after making some changes to speed up playing.

We previously received feedback that it was too hard to run out of mana, and that it didn't have a real limiting effect. After making some changes, 60% of players said they did run out of mana.

Overall, everyone surveyed found the game at least moderately fun.

Some other comments we received included confusion over what mana actually is (ie, the terminology was not clearly explained in the instructions), not being sure whether they would be able to cast (due to lack of mana or cool down period), that not all spells were equally useful, and one report of spamming spells being an issue.
